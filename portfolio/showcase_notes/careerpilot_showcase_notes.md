# CareerPilotAgent Showcase Notes

## Core Story

A privacy-first career workflow for international Business and Marketing students: analyse a job description, understand fit, tailor evidence honestly, prepare a draft, track the opportunity, and export a report.

## Public Demo Rules

- Use Demo Profile only.
- Use synthetic job descriptions from `data/sample_jobs.csv`.
- Keep the tracker empty or clearly synthetic.
- Never display `data/private/user_profile.json`.
- Capture screenshots only after running tests and the public safety check.

## Suggested Walkthrough

1. Show the hero and privacy boundary.
2. Open the read-only Demo Profile.
3. Load the Business Analyst sample job.
4. Explain the seven score dimensions and honest risk notes.
5. Show project-based resume suggestions and the overclaim warning.
6. Show the editable cover-letter draft.
7. Show the local tracker without private notes.
8. Finish with the structured Markdown report.
